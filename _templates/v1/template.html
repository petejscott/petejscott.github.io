About the Author

My name is Pete Scott. I've been writing shitty code since 1996 and practicing writing code well since 2011.

I'm a software architect who believes that a software architect has three responsibilities: make decisions about things that won't be easy to change, make as many things easy to change as possible, and help everybody else become an architect.

I'm a ScrumMaster who is passionate about agile principles, lean methodologies, learning, thinking, and iteratively improving complex systems.

I'm a fan of coffee and scotch, linquistics, and my family (who help to keep me focused on what really matters).
